PRIVACY POLICY

At Staery Sky PH, we respect and value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect:

  • When you place an order or create an account, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information.
  • We may also collect information about your device, browsing actions, and usage patterns to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • To process your orders and provide customer support.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, promotions, and updates.
  • To improve our website and tailor your shopping experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Sharing Your Information:

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We may share your information with trusted partners (e.g., payment processors, shipping companies) to fulfill your order.
  • In the case of legal requirements or investigations, we may disclose your information as necessary.

4. Security of Your Information:

  • We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information.
  • While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights:

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us at contactssph@gmail.com for assistance.
  • You may opt-out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in our emails.

6. Changes to This Policy:

  • We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

For questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contactssph@gmail.com.
